Integration Overview

Your Snowflake Data, Connected to Everything

Data Integration is the foundational entry point into Domo — establishing the unified, governed data layer your Snowflake environment needs to power analytics, AI, automation, and action across every team.

Feed Snowflake from Anywhere

Connect 1,000+ sources — cloud apps, databases, ERPs, flat files, and APIs — directly into your Snowflake warehouse. Batch, micro-batch, and streaming all supported. Incremental ingestion keeps costs low and data fresh.

Connect Snowflake to the Rest of Your Stack

Native Snowflake connectors let Domo visualize, analyze, and distribute Snowflake data in real-time — with write-back capabilities to push decisions and enriched data back to your warehouse or source systems.

Governance That Inherits from Snowflake

Row- and column-level permissions can be inherited directly from your Snowflake warehouse. SOC 2, HIPAA, and GDPR compliance built in — with tamper-proof audit logs and automated PII monitoring across every pipeline.

Event-Based Pipeline Triggers

Flat File & Document Ingestion

Versioned Sandbox Environments
